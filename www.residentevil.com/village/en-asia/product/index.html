Resident Evil Re:Verse included for free!

Game Details

Resident Evil Re:Verse, the online-only multiplayer game that lets you battle it out as beloved characters from the Resident Evil series, will be available for free for anyone that has bought either Resident Evil Village or Resident Evil Village Gold Edition!

Limited Bonus

Bonus Content

The Street Wolf Outfit for use in Shadows of Rose

An alternative outfit that Rosemary Winters can wear in the new story included with the Winters' Expansion, Shadows of Rose.
The outfit bears the emblem of Chris Redfield's elite Hound Wolf unit.

  • *In order to use this content, you must download the Winters' Expansion.
  • *Those who purchase the physical edition of Resident Evil Village Gold Edition can acquire this content via the product code included with the game, whereas those who purchase the digital edition can download the content through their relevant platform's online store.
  • *The limited bonus for the digital version on consoles will be available for pre-orders made before October 27, 2022 (Thurs) 14:59 UTC. On Steam, the limited bonus will be given to those who purchase Resident Evil Village Gold Edition before November 24th, 2022 (Thurs) 16:59 UTC, or purchase the Winters' Expansion before October 27, 2022 (Thurs) 14:59 UTC.
  • *The limited bonus may become available through other means in the future.
  • *Please be aware that physical editions of the game including the limited bonus will only be available in limited numbers.
  • *The product code can only be redeemed once, and the ability to connect to the PlayStation Network or Xbox Live is required in order to acquire the content.
  • *The product code included with either the PlayStation®4 or PlayStation®5 versions will allow you to download the content for both consoles. Similarly, the product code included with the Xbox One or Xbox Series X|S versions will allow you to use the limited bonus on any of those consoles.
  • *Contents and specifications are subject to change without prior notice.
  • *Images are used for illustrative purposes.

Points to Note Regarding Resident Evil Re:Verse

  • *If you purchase Resident Evil Village on the PlayStation®5, then the version of Resident Evil Re:Verse will be the PlayStation®4 one, and if you purchase Village on the Xbox Series X, you will receive the Xbox One version of Re:Verse.
  • *Resident Evil Re:Verse is an online-only multiplayer title. The PlayStation®4 and PlayStation®5 versions require a PlayStation®Plus membership, and the Xbox One and Xbox Series X versions require an Xbox Live Gold membership in order to play.
  • *Details of the operational period for Resident Evil Re:Verse, including commencement of availability and any potential announcements relating to the end of service, can be found on the official website.
  • *Please note that, depending on the timing of your purchase of Resident Evil Village, Resident Evil Re:Verse may already no longer be available, and/or the title may become available through other means in the future.
  • *Product codes are included with the physical version of Resident Evil Village. If you purchase the digital version, content can be downloaded from the online store the game was purchased from.
  • *Product codes will become invalid if the content is no longer available.
