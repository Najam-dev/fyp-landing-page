How to Play
the Mini Soundtrack

Before you use the mini soundtrack, please have a suitable USB storage device ready.

  • 1. Once installed, start the mini soundtrack.
  • 2. Follow the on-screen instructions and copy the soundtrack to the USB storage device. Once it has finished copying, please close the mini soundtrack.
  • 3. Insert the USB storage device to your PS4 or PS5 to play it.
  • Note: There may be instances where the mini soundtrack on the USB device is not recognized. Please re-insert the USB device and try again.

Please see "Listening to music on a USB storage device" for more details on how to listen to the mini soundtrack. (Skip steps 1 and 2.)
You can play the mini soundtrack from the Music icon on the PS5.
